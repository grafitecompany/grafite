Our Work



Through its fundraising efforts - GoFundMe, Pool Tournaments, Minecraft PvP Tournaments, and Bake Sales - Grafite has raised approximately $5300 of money and supplies to donate to various groups.



Dog Treat Sale

Over the summer Grafite created a new fundraiser not for people, but for their pets! A dog treat sale nearby the New Providence Community Pool and High School allowed us to connect more with the community while also providing a unique product and raising funds for donations.





PS12 Jersey City School

Grafite has worked extensively with Julia A. Barnes Public Elementary School (PS12) in Jersey City. We have donated school supplies to their public supply drives for the community. Our officers have also worked closely in person with PS12 students in 3rd and 4th grade during Saturday events. We've tutored basic algebra and taught about the Engineering Design Process. We even hosted an Engineering challenge in which students were tasked with creating a contraption to move an object into a target area, given limited resources and time. We are still in contact with PS12 and can't wait to continue working with them in the future!





EMS Services Donation

During the pandemic, Grafite temporarily shifted its donation infrastructure towards Covid relief efforts. As part of this effort, Grafite raised funds for and donated "care packages" of snacks and other basic supplies to New Providence Emergency Medical Service (EMS) responders.




Morristown Memorial Hospital Donation

Grafite donated similar care packages to grateful nursing staff at the Morristown Memorial Hospital. We also donated several hundred N95 and surgical masks.
